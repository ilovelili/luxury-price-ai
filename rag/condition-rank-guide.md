# Condition Rank Guide

Use this guide to explain item condition in luxury appraisal drafts. Treat rank as a practical appraisal signal, not as a final truth. Photos and staff confirmation can change the final rank.

## Core Rule

Do not decide price from condition wording alone. Use the pricing API for market price and purchase-offer ranges. Use this guide only to describe condition risks, missing evidence, and likely staff review points.

## Rank Vocabulary

The auction data and the pricing API use this full rank set, from best to worst condition:

`S`, `N`, `A`, `AB`, `B`, `BC`, `C`, `D`, `F`

`S` and `N` are treated as the same top tier. Both user-provided ranks and comparable-sale ranks can use any of these values, so every value below has draft wording. Do not invent ranks outside this set, and do not collapse `BC`, `D`, or `F` into a neighbour without saying so.

## Rank Definitions

### S / N: 新品・未使用

Use when the item appears unused or nearly unused and has no visible wear. `S` and `N` are the same tier; `N` typically means 新品同様 / 未使用に近い.

Typical signals:

- No visible scratches, stains, corner wear, or hardware tarnish.
- Shape is clean and not collapsed.
- Protective seals, tags, box, dust bag, receipt, warranty card, or full accessories may be present.

Draft wording:

- 「画像上は使用感がほとんど確認できず、未使用に近い状態として確認できる可能性があります。」
- 「付属品の有無と保管状態の確認後、最終ランクを判断します。」

### A: ほぼ新品

Use when the item has very light signs of handling but remains clean overall.

Typical signals:

- Very minor scratches or slight hardware marks.
- Very light leather creasing.
- No obvious stains, strong odors, peeling, or structural deformation.

Draft wording:

- 「全体的にきれいな状態ですが、金具や表面にごく軽微な使用感が見られる場合があります。」

### AB: 使用感少ない

Use when normal light use is visible but the item remains attractive for resale.

Typical signals:

- Small scratches, slight corner wear, light chain or hardware tarnish.
- Minor interior marks or light leather creasing.
- Shape remains mostly intact.

Draft wording:

- 「軽い使用感はありますが、再販上大きなマイナスになりにくい範囲と考えられます。」
- 「角スレや金具小傷の程度により、ABからB相当での確認が必要です。」

### B: 使用感あり

Use when wear is clearly visible and affects resale appeal.

Typical signals:

- Noticeable scratches, stains, corner wear, interior dirt, discoloration, or odor risk.
- Hardware tarnish or plating wear.
- Shape collapse or visible deformation.

Draft wording:

- 「画像上、使用感が確認できるため、状態評価は慎重に行う必要があります。」
- 「角スレ・汚れ・型崩れの程度により、買取提示レンジは下振れする可能性があります。」

### BC: 使用感はっきり

Use when wear is between `B` and `C`: clearly more than light use, but not yet heavy damage.

Typical signals:

- Multiple visible scratches, corner wear, or interior dirt beyond `B`.
- Noticeable hardware tarnish or plating wear.
- Shape softening but not collapsed, no major structural damage.

Draft wording:

- 「使用感がはっきりと確認できるため、BからC寄りの状態として慎重に評価する必要があります。」
- 「角スレ・汚れ・金具の劣化の程度により、買取提示レンジは下振れする可能性があります。」

### C: 古い・ダメージあり

Use when damage is significant or repair/maintenance may be required.

Typical signals:

- Tears, peeling, sticky interior, broken zipper, missing parts, heavy stains, strong odor, mold risk, or severe deformation.
- Serial label is missing or unreadable when it should exist.

Draft wording:

- 「ダメージが強く見られる場合は、通常相場より大きく下振れする可能性があります。」
- 「修理可否、真贋確認、付属品確認を含めてスタッフ確認が必要です。」

### D: ダメージ大

Use when damage is heavy enough that resale value is limited and repair is likely required.

Typical signals:

- Strong overall wear, large stains, peeling, structural deformation, or multiple damaged areas.
- Function-affecting issues such as broken zipper, broken clasp, or detached parts.

Draft wording:

- 「ダメージが大きいため、相場より大幅に下振れする、または買取対象外となる可能性があります。」
- 「修理可否・真贋・付属品を含め、スタッフによる実物確認が前提です。」

### F: ジャンク・難あり

Use for junk-grade items with severe damage, missing core parts, or unclear resale viability.

Typical signals:

- Severe damage, heavy mold, strong odor, broken structure, or major missing parts.
- Resale viability is uncertain and depends on staff judgement.

Draft wording:

- 「状態が大きく損なわれているため、現時点では価格レンジの提示を控え、スタッフ確認を前提とした参考扱いとします。」
- 「買取可否を含めて実物確認が必要です。」

## Photo Evidence Priority

Ask for these angles when condition is uncertain:

- Front
- Back
- Four corners
- Inside and serial/inside label
- Hardware and chain
- Damage spots
- Accessories, box, dust bag, guarantee card, receipt

## Important Cautions

- Do not guarantee authenticity from photos.
- Do not overstate rank from one image.
- If photos are blurry, dark, cropped, or missing key angles, say staff confirmation is needed.
- If rank from user input conflicts with visible damage, mention that final rank may change after review.
