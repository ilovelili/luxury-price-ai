# Photo Review Checklist

Use this checklist to guide image-based appraisal notes. Images are supporting evidence only. Do not estimate price directly from images.

## Required Photos By Category

### ブランドバッグ / bags

Ask for at least:

- Front
- Back
- Four corners
- Bottom
- Inside
- Serial label, IC plate, or authenticity marker
- Hardware, chain, zipper, lock
- Handle and strap
- Damage spots
- Accessories: box, dust bag, card, receipt

### 財布・小物 / small-goods

Ask for:

- Front
- Back
- Corners
- Interior card slots
- Coin pocket
- Zipper or snap
- Serial/IC/authenticity marker if available
- Accessories

### 時計 / watches

Ask for:

- Dial/front
- Case back
- Crown
- Bracelet/belt
- Clasp
- Serial/reference area if visible
- Warranty card
- Box and extra links
- Scratch or damage close-ups

### アクセサリー / accessories

Ask for:

- Full item
- Hallmark/stamp
- Clasp
- Stone close-up
- Damage or deformation
- Box, certificate, receipt

### 貴金属 / precious-metals

Ask for:

- Full item
- Material stamp such as K18, Pt900, Pt850, 925
- Weight if known
- Stone close-up
- Ring size or chain length
- Certificate,鑑定書, box, receipt

### 服 / apparel

Ask for:

- Front
- Back
- Brand tag
- Size/material tag
- Collar, cuffs, hem, underarm areas
- Stains, holes, fraying, alterations

### スニーカー・靴 / shoes

Ask for:

- Front
- Side
- Back/heel
- Sole
- Inside size tag
- Box label
- Accessories such as spare laces or dust bags

### 陶器・食器 / ceramics

Ask for:

- Full item or full set
- Maker mark
- Bottom
- Chips/cracks close-up
- Box and certificate

### カメラ / cameras

Ask for:

- Front body
- Back screen
- Lens glass
- Mount
- Serial/model label
- Battery compartment
- Accessories

### 美術品・骨董品 / art

Ask for:

- Full front
- Back
- Signature or stamp
- Frame/base
- Certificate/provenance
- Damage or repair close-ups

### 家具 / furniture

Ask for:

- Full front
- Side and back
- Brand/designer mark
- Dimensions
- Scratches, stains, fabric/leather damage
- Joints, legs, and underside

### 自転車 / bicycles

Ask for:

- Full bike side view
- Brand/model
- Frame serial
- Drivetrain
- Wheels and tires
- Brakes
- Damage or rust close-ups

### 家電 / home-appliances

Ask for:

- Full item
- Model/serial label
- Accessories
- Operation screen or power-on state
- Damage close-ups

### ホビー / hobbies

Ask for:

- Full item
- Box
- Included parts
- Limited edition markings
- Damage or missing-part close-ups

### スポーツ用品 / sports

Ask for:

- Full item
- Brand/model/spec label
- Grip/contact areas
- Wear or crack close-ups
- Accessories

### 楽器 / instruments

Ask for:

- Full front
- Back
- Headstock or brand mark
- Serial/model label
- Case/accessories
- Crack, neck, bridge, electronics, or key close-ups

### ゲーム / games

Ask for:

- Console/software front
- Back
- Model/serial label
- Box/manual/accessories
- Operation screen if possible

### パソコン・PC / computers

Ask for:

- Full device
- Screen powered on
- Model/serial label
- Keyboard/ports
- Battery or system information if available
- Charger/accessories

### キャンプ用品 / camping

Ask for:

- Full item assembled if possible
- Brand/model label
- Fabric, poles, zipper, seams
- Missing parts or damage close-ups

### オーディオ / audio

Ask for:

- Full item
- Model/serial label
- Drivers/speaker cones
- Ports/cables
- Remote/accessories
- Operation state if possible

## Condition Signals To Extract

Only mention what is visible:

- Scratches
- Corner wear
- Stains
- Discoloration
- Shape collapse
- Leather creasing
- Peeling
- Sticky interior risk
- Hardware tarnish
- Plating wear
- Chain wear
- Missing parts
- Broken zipper or snap
- Odor risk only if user states it; do not infer odor from images

## Missing Photo Angles

If key angles are missing, ask for them directly:

- 「正面・背面・四つ角・内側・金具・シリアル/ICプレートの写真を追加いただけると、状態確認の精度が上がります。」

If uploaded photos are blurry:

- 「一部画像が不鮮明なため、傷や汚れの程度は実物確認または追加写真での確認が必要です。」

If lighting changes color:

- 「照明の影響で色味判断が難しいため、自然光での追加写真があると確認しやすくなります。」

## Vision Output Rules

The appraisal draft may say:

- Visible model/material/color/hardware clues
- Visible condition risks
- Missing angles
- Suggested condition rank only when clearly supported

The appraisal draft must not say:

- The item is authentic
- The final purchase price is guaranteed
- Damage exists when it is not visible or stated
- A precise rank when photos are incomplete

## Suggested Photo Request Template

「より正確に確認するため、追加で以下の写真をご共有ください。正面、背面、四つ角、内側、シリアル/ICプレート、金具、ダメージ箇所、付属品一式。」
